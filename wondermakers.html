FAQ

What kinds of projects are a good fit for Wonder Makers?

↓
Our core areas include high-end websites and digital experiences, applications and SaaS platforms, premium e-commerce, and Web3 or AI products. We also take on projects where design and engineering create a real advantage. We work with both global enterprises and early-stage startups.

What does a project like this typically cost?

↓
It depends on scope and complexity. Focused projects start from $10,000. Full web presence typically ranges $40,000 - $150,000. End-to-end product delivery starts from $80,000.

How do you approach pricing and project models?

↓
We offer fixed-price models for well-specified projects, structured options for medium complexity, and flexible agile models for evolving scopes. Every project is different, and we aim to stay flexible while setting clear expectations.

We're working with a tight deadline – how fast can you move?

↓
Faster than most studios. Typical timelines: Landing page as little as 14 days. Full web presence: 6–10 weeks. Digital product: 3–6 months. Rush timelines are possible.

Will we actually work with the senior team we meet at the start?

↓
Yes. Wonder Makers is a senior-led studio, which means the team you meet during the first conversation is the team that works on your project. We don't pitch with seniors and deliver with juniors.

How is your team structured, and how do you handle international collaboration?

↓
We are a Prague-based studio of 20+ in-house specialists, working with clients globally. Remote collaboration is a natural part of how we operate. Our CET timezone allows us to overlap effectively across regions.

Can you work alongside our internal team?

↓
Yes – this is one of our core engagement models. Through Embedded Expertise, our senior specialists integrate directly into your team, using your tools, joining your standups, and strengthening specific workstreams.

We don't have a clear brief yet – can we still start?

↓
Yes – we can start with a Discovery & Definition phase, where we help shape the brief, validate key assumptions, and define a clear roadmap for delivery. This is especially important for complex projects.

Do you help shape the product, or mainly execute on briefs?

↓
Strategy is the first service we offer – not an add-on. Our Product Strategy work includes discovery, user research, competitive analysis, and roadmapping. In many cases, we help refine the problem before building the solution.

Is your team experienced enough to handle complex, large-scale technical projects?

↓
Yes – this is a core part of our work. We design and build scalable backend architectures, real-time platforms, SaaS products, multi-chain Web3 infrastructure. What sets us apart is combining that level of engineering with high-end design.

How do you approach AI in your work?

↓
Selectively and transparently. In design, generative tools help accelerate early concept exploration. In development, we use AI to support code generation and rapid prototyping. AI never replaces senior judgment.

What happens after launch?

↓
Many clients continue working with us after launch through ongoing product development. This may include feature development, performance optimisation, analytics, or iterative UX improvements.
